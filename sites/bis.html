It will be a free workshop during Udbhav ,Organized by CESA on October 18, 2024.This workshop is about HOW TO USE IS CODES ,is aimed at providing engineers with a thorough understanding of Indian standards relevant to their field.The workshop focuses on the correct interpretation and practical application of IS CODES in engineering design , construction and quality control.
It equips engineers with the knowledge needed to ensure compliance with national standards and safety.Through real world examples and case studies, participants would be able to learn how to effectively use IS Codes in their professional practice

*Key points :-
1. .Introduction to Standards: Explaining what standards are, how they are developed, and why they are important for industry and society.
2. Standardization in Various Fields: Overview of standards related to engineering, technology, environmental management, construction, electronics, and consumer goods.
3. Laboratory Testing and Quality Assurance: Understanding how testing and quality control play a role in standardization.
4. Career Opportunities in Standards and Quality: Introducing students to potential careers in the field of standardization, quality control, and testing.
