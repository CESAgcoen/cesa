Waste to Wonders:

Waste to Wonders

Event Coordinator :-
Payal Atmande - 8788465449
Gaurav Bisen - 8698378255

Date: 18th October
- Requirements:
1. Open to all age groups (individuals).
2. Each entry must incorporate at least three different types of waste or recycled materials.

Rules :
- Each participant can submit only one entry.
- Ensure all materials used are safe and non-toxic.
Evaluation Criteria:

Projects will be judged based on:
- Creativity: Uniqueness and originality of the idea.
- Functionality: Practical use or aesthetic appeal.
- Sustainability: Effective use of materials and environmental impact.

Presentation:
Participants will present their projects on event day, highlighting the materials used and the creative process behind their work.

Enrollment fees : Rs.50
